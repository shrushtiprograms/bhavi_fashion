Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2025

Notice: This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

1. Information Collection

We collect various types of information to provide better services to our customers, enhance your shopping experience, and ensure the smooth operation of our website. The information we collect falls into several categories:

Personal Information

This includes information that can be used to identify you personally, which we collect when you interact with our services, such as:

  • Full name, email address, phone number, and other contact details provided during account creation or order placement.
  • Billing and shipping addresses to process and deliver your orders accurately.
  • Payment information, such as credit/debit card details or UPI IDs, which are securely processed through our payment gateways.
  • Account credentials, including usernames and encrypted passwords, to secure your account.
  • Custom order details, such as measurements, fabric preferences, and design specifications for tailored products.
  • Customer service interactions, including emails, chat logs, or call recordings (with your consent), to resolve inquiries or disputes.
Technical Information

We collect non-personal technical data to improve website functionality and user experience, including:

  • IP address, which helps us understand your geographic location and secure our services.
  • Device information, such as device type, operating system, screen resolution, and unique device identifiers.
  • Browser information, including browser type, version, language preferences, and settings.
  • Cookies and usage data, such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and click patterns, to analyze user behavior.
  • Location data (if enabled), derived from your IP address or GPS, to offer location-based services like shipping estimates.
  • Referral data, such as the website or advertisement that directed you to our site, to evaluate marketing campaigns.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for various purposes to operate our business, improve our services, and provide a personalized experience. Below are the key ways we utilize your data:

Purpose Examples
Order Processing Fulfilling purchases, processing payments securely, coordinating with artisans for custom orders, generating invoices, tracking shipments, and sending delivery updates.
Customer Service Responding to inquiries via email, phone, or chat; handling returns, refunds, or exchanges; resolving disputes; and providing support for custom order clarifications.
Website Improvements Analyzing user behavior to optimize website navigation, improve product recommendations, enhance search functionality, and develop new features based on feedback.
Marketing & Promotions Sending personalized offers, discounts, or newsletters (with your consent); displaying targeted ads based on your browsing history; and notifying you about new product launches or seasonal sales.
Security & Fraud Prevention Detecting and preventing fraudulent activities, monitoring suspicious transactions, ensuring compliance with legal requirements, and protecting your account from unauthorized access.
Legal Compliance Retaining data as required by tax laws, responding to legal requests from authorities, and maintaining records for auditing purposes.

3. Data Sharing & Protection

We may share your information with trusted third parties to facilitate our services, but we do not sell or rent your data for marketing purposes. The entities we share with include:

  • Payment Processors: To securely handle transactions, such as Stripe or Razorpay, ensuring compliance with PCI-DSS standards.
  • Shipping Partners: To deliver your orders, such as India Post, DHL, or FedEx, sharing only necessary details like your address and contact number.
  • Artisans/Tailors: For custom orders, we share measurements and design preferences with artisans, ensuring minimal data exposure.
  • Cloud Service Providers: To store data securely, using providers like AWS or Google Cloud, with strict access controls.
  • Marketing Partners: To manage email campaigns or ads (with your consent), such as Mailchimp or Google Ads, ensuring your data is anonymized where possible.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, such as in response to a court order, subpoena, or regulatory investigation.
  • Business Partners: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity, with prior notice to you.

We take extensive measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transfers between your browser and our servers, ensuring secure communication.
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment processing to safeguard your financial information during transactions.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments conducted by third-party experts to identify and mitigate risks.
  • Limited employee access to sensitive data, with role-based permissions and mandatory training on data privacy.
  • Firewall protection and intrusion detection systems to prevent unauthorized access to our servers.
  • Data anonymization for analytics purposes, ensuring your identity is not linked to usage data.
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery plans to ensure data availability in case of unexpected events.

Despite these measures, no online system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and avoid sharing your account details.

4. Your Rights

As a user, you have several rights regarding your personal data, which we are committed to upholding in compliance with applicable laws:

Right to Access

You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, including order history, account details, and marketing preferences.

Right to Rectify

If your information is inaccurate or incomplete, you can request updates to your name, address, or contact details through your account or by contacting us.

Right to Delete

You can request the erasure of your data, subject to legal retention requirements (e.g., tax records), such as deleting your account and associated data.

Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format to transfer to another service provider.

Right to Object

You can object to certain uses of your data, such as marketing emails, by opting out via email links or contacting us directly.

Right to Restrict Processing

You can request that we limit the processing of your data while we verify its accuracy or address your concerns.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@bhaviindia.com. We may require identity verification to process your request.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site performance, and deliver personalized content:

Cookie Types

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Strictly Necessary: Essential for website functionality, such as maintaining your session during checkout.
  • Performance: Collect data on how users interact with our site, helping us improve performance and fix issues.
  • Functionality: Enable features like remembering your preferences (e.g., language or currency).
  • Targeting: Used for advertising purposes, such as showing you relevant ads based on your browsing history.
Management

You can adjust your cookie preferences directly through your browser settings, such as enabling or disabling cookies, or clearing existing cookies. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
