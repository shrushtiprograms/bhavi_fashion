Return Policy

At Bhavi India Fashion, we strive to ensure your satisfaction with every purchase. This Return Policy outlines the conditions under which you can return or exchange products.

1. Eligibility for Returns

Items are eligible for return or exchange if they meet the following criteria:

  • The item is unused, unwashed, and in its original packaging with tags attached.
  • The return is initiated within 7 days of delivery.
  • The item is not a custom or personalized product (e.g., custom designs or tailored orders).

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Custom-made or personalized products.
  • Items damaged due to misuse or improper care.
  • Underwear, accessories, or consumable products.

3. Return Process

To initiate a return, follow these steps:

  1. Contact us at support@bhaviindia.com with your order number and reason for return within 7 days of delivery.
  2. Receive a return authorization and shipping instructions from our team.
  3. Ship the item back to the provided address at your expense.
  4. Once received and inspected, we will process your refund or exchange.

4. Refunds

Upon approval, refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business days. Refund amounts exclude shipping costs unless the return is due to our error (e.g., defective item).

5. Exchanges

If you prefer an exchange, we will ship the replacement item after receiving and inspecting the returned product, subject to availability.

6. Contact Us

For any questions about returns, please reach out to:

  • Email: support@bhaviindia.com
  • Phone: +91 1234567890
